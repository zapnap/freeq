## Validation Criteria

- The "Sign in manually" button never appears solely because the broker took >15 s
  to respond once. Verified by simulating a slow broker and observing the
  reconnect retry chain does not surface the manual-signin button until the
  reconnect attempt has truly failed (≥ 45 s total).
- Tapping "Sign in manually" no longer drops broker credentials. After the tap
  the user is taken to ConnectView; if they back out, the saved session is
  still there and resumes on next app launch.
- The `reconnectSeconds` timer in `ContentView` resets at the start of each
  reconnect attempt, not from first `.onAppear`.

## Motivation

`ContentView.swift:68-83`: `reconnectSeconds` starts ticking from the very first
`.onAppear` of `reconnectingView`, not when each fresh reconnect attempt begins.
Combined with `fetchBrokerSession`'s 4-retry inner loop and the outer
exponential backoff in `reconnectSavedSession`, the user can easily hit 15 s
mid-retry — sees the cliff, taps "Sign in manually", and — although credentials
are NOT auto-cleared — the user thinks they are and re-OAuths anyway because
that's what the UI said to do.
